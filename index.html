Air Source Heat Pump Installation in Aberdeen

An air source heat pump (ASHP) extracts heat from the outside air — even in temperatures as low as -20°C — and uses it to heat your home and hot water. It works like a fridge in reverse, and because it moves heat rather than generates it, it produces far more energy than it consumes.

For Aberdeen homeowners, air source heat pumps are the most popular and cost-effective route to low-carbon heating. They require no groundworks, can usually be installed in one to two days, and are compatible with most existing central heating systems.

Not all installers are equal. Every installation we carry out is done by fully MCS certified engineers — meaning you qualify for the Home Energy Scotland Grant and your system meets the highest industry standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to install a heat pump in Scotland?

Air source heat pump installation typically costs between £8,000 and £15,000 depending on the size of your home and the system required. Most Aberdeen homeowners qualify for the Home Energy Scotland Grant of up to £7,500, which significantly reduces the upfront cost. An optional interest-free loan of up to £7,500 is also available.

Is it worth getting a heat pump in Scotland?

Yes — Scotland is one of the best places in the UK for heat pumps. Over 85% of Scotland's electricity comes from renewable sources, making heat pumps even greener here than elsewhere. Combined with the Home Energy Scotland Grant and lower running costs, the financial and environmental case is compelling.

Who is eligible for the heat pump grant in Scotland?

Any homeowner in Scotland can apply for the Home Energy Scotland Grant — you do not need to be on benefits. The main requirements are that you own your home, it is an existing property (not a new build), the heat pump provides 100% of your heating and hot water, and it is installed by an MCS certified installer.

What is the lifespan of a heat pump?

A well-maintained heat pump typically lasts 20–25 years, significantly longer than a gas boiler. Annual servicing keeps the system running efficiently and protects the manufacturer warranty.

Are heat pumps efficient in cold weather?

Yes. Modern heat pumps are built to handle Scottish winters. During the January 2025 cold snap, 9 in 10 heat pump owners reported strong performance in freezing and snowy conditions.

Does my gas meter need to be removed as part of the install?

No. Your gas meter does not need to be removed when a heat pump is installed. Some homeowners choose to close their gas account over time to remove the standing charge, but this is entirely optional.
