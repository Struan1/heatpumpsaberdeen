Heat Pump Grants Available in Inverurie

Heat Pumps Aberdeen are your local, MCS certified specialists for heat pump installation in Inverurie and the surrounding area. We install air source and ground source heat pumps, handle the Home Energy Scotland Grant application on your behalf, and remain on hand for servicing and aftercare long after your installation is complete.

Most Inverurie homeowners qualify for the Home Energy Scotland Grant — up to £7,500 towards your installation, with no requirement to be on benefits. Rural Aberdeenshire properties can receive up to £9,000. An optional interest-free loan of up to £7,500 is also available, repayable over up to 10 years at 0%.

If you're on a low income or certain qualifying benefits, you may also qualify for Warmer Homes Scotland, which can cover the full cost of installation. We'll confirm your eligibility as part of your free survey.
