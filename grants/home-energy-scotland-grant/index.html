Home Energy Scotland Grant & Loan

A Scottish Government scheme providing homeowners with a free grant — and an optional interest-free loan — towards the cost of installing a heat pump. Available to all Aberdeen and Aberdeenshire homeowners regardless of income.

How Much Can You Get?

  • Up to £7,500 as a free grant — money you never have to pay back
  • Up to £9,000 grant for rural and island Aberdeenshire homes
  • Optional interest-free loan of up to £7,500 — repayable over up to 10 years at 0%
  • Combined maximum of £15,000 (or £16,500 for rural homes) in grant and loan funding

Do You Qualify?

  • You own your home and it is your primary residence in Scotland
  • The property is an existing building — not a new build
  • The heat pump will provide 100% of your heating and hot water
  • Your installer is MCS certified
  • You have a valid EPC or Home Energy Scotland assessment recommending the improvement

You do not need to be on benefits to qualify. Funding is allocated on a first-come, first-served basis, so it's worth applying sooner rather than later.

How to Apply

01

Contact HES

Call 0808 808 2282 (free, Mon–Fri 8am–8pm, Sat 9am–5pm) or apply via the Home Energy Scotland website.

02

Eligibility Check

An advisor confirms your eligibility and refers you to the grant scheme.

03

Funding Offer

You receive a written funding offer. Do not start any work until you have this in hand.

04

Installation & Claim

Complete your installation within 9 months and claim your grant funding.

As your MCS certified installer, Heat Pumps Aberdeen will guide you through every step of this process and ensure all documentation is in order for your claim.

Not sure if you qualify? You may also be eligible for Warmer Homes Scotland if you are on a low income or qualifying benefits.
