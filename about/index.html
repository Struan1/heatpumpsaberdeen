Who We Are

Heat Pumps Aberdeen are a local team of MCS certified renewable energy specialists serving Aberdeen and the surrounding towns of Aberdeenshire. We install air source heat pumps, ground source heat pumps, solar panels and battery storage systems — helping homeowners across the region cut their energy bills, reduce their carbon footprint and future-proof their homes.

We started Heat Pumps Aberdeen because we believe that switching to renewable heating should be simple, honest and stress-free. Too many homeowners are put off by confusing information, pushy sales tactics or national companies that don't understand local properties. We're here to change that. Every survey is free, every quote is transparent and we'll only ever recommend a system that's genuinely right for your home.

As MCS certified installers, every installation we carry out qualifies for the Home Energy Scotland Grant — and we handle the entire application process on your behalf. From your first call through to aftercare and servicing, we're with you every step of the way.

We're proud to be local. We know Aberdeenshire homes, we understand the climate and we're on hand when you need us — not a call centre hundreds of miles away.
